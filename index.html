BloodConnect Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BloodConnect, Cameroon’s first mobile blood donation app. Your trust is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users:

  • Personal details such as your full name, phone number, and email address.
  • Medical details such as blood group and eligibility to donate.
  • Location data to connect recipients with nearby donors.
  • Verification documents (e.g., ID, blood group proof) if submitted for donor verification.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Connect blood recipients with suitable and available donors.
  • Send important notifications about requests and responses.
  • Ensure donor authenticity and build trust through verification.
  • Improve app functionality, security, and user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Your information is shared only when necessary, such as:

  • With recipients who need a donor, limited to essential contact details.
  • With hospitals or medical authorities when required for safety or compliance.
  • With third-party services (like Firebase or Cloudinary) for secure storage and operations.

5. Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously. BloodConnect uses secure databases, encryption, and authentication to safeguard your information. However, no system is completely secure, and users are encouraged to exercise caution when sharing sensitive information.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information in the app.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of notifications at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

BloodConnect is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app means you accept these changes.
